Understanding HTML Semantics

Date: June 09, 2025

Today, we will explore some semantic elements of HTML.

Semantic elements give meaning to your page, making it easier for browsers and developers to read.

“HTML without semantics is like a document without structure.”

Let’s look at an example of code:

<section>
  <article>
    <h2>Blog Title</h2>
    <p>Blog content...</p>
  </article>
</section>

Remember to use meaningful tags like <header>, <article>, and <footer>.
